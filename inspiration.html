My Inspiration

This page is dedicated to things that inspire me both personally and professionally

NASA & SpaceX

Space exploration has always captured my imagination. From growing up on shows like Star Trek and Star Wars I always dreamed of what we might discover through our aerospace initiatives. I follow both NASA and SpaceX very closely on their projects. I'm captivated by the Voyager mission, our current Mars mission, and the Orion development for our future human space travelers.


NASA's Path to Mars

Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality has been a dream of computer scientists for several decades and arguably before computers themselves. Much of the technical hurdles that have kept it from the mainstream are just now being solved and these barriers are disappearing. I have always dreamed of being able to experience that sense of presence in a virtual world. This entire industry inspires me to better learn programming, and the development side of virtual reality.


Mark Zuckerberg on why virtual reality is the next big thing

John Carmack

As someone who grew up gaming on the PC, games like Wolfenstein, Doom, and Quake shaped how I viewed games and even graphics use on the PC in general. The innovations that came out of id software were spearheaded by John Carmack. He is currently the CTO of Oculus, the virtual reality company owned by Facebook.
